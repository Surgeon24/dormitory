How It Works

- Each student can reserve up to 2 hours per day per facility.
- Cancellations should be made at least 30 minutes in advance.
- Facilities must be left clean and ready for others.

Need help? Contact the dorm manager at the first floor or visit the front desk.

What happens if I don't use my reservation?

In case of a single non-use of the reservation you will get a warning. Multiple non-use of your own reservation may result in the blocking of this facility for you.

Do I have to pay for the reservation?

All facilities in our dorms are absolutely free for their residents.
